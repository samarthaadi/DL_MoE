Router Architecture Variants — MLP vs GRU vs CNN
R8 replaces the per-token MLP with a BiGRU that sees the full word sequence, enabling left-right context in routing decisions. R9 uses a 1D CNN (kernel=5, ±2-word window) capturing local neighbourhood context. All three share τ=1.0 and soft token-level routing with both expert hidden states as input.
